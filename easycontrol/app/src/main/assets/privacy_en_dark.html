Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-08

1. App Purpose

EasyControl is used to connect, control, and mirror Android devices that you authorize. The app may use USB, wireless ADB, or local network connections to provide screen mirroring, app transfer, audio transfer, and input control.

2. Data Used By This App

To complete connection and mirroring features, the app may store device names, device UUIDs, connection addresses, ports, mirroring parameters, display settings, ADB keys, logs, and app package names that you configure manually. These data are stored locally by default and are not actively uploaded to developer servers.

3. Permission Usage

4. Audio, Video, And Control Data

During mirroring, screen frames, audio, and touch/key commands are transmitted between this device and the controlled device you connect. The app does not actively send these contents to the developer or unrelated third parties. Please only connect devices you own or are authorized to use.

5. Local Storage And Clearing

You can clear this app's data in system settings, or regenerate ADB keys inside the app. After clearing data, saved devices, authorization state, settings, and keys may need to be configured again.

6. Your Choice

On first launch, tap "Agree and continue" to confirm that you have read and accepted this policy. Tap "Cancel and exit" to close the app.
